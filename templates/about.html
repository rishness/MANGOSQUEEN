About MangosQueen

Your Companion for Smart Mangosteen Fruit Detection and Sorting

Our Mission

MangosQueen is dedicated to revolutionizing mangosteen cultivation and production through cutting-edge technology. Our platform combines advanced image recognition, machine learning, and agricultural expertise to help farmers and producers identify diseases early, implement effective prevention strategies, and optimize fruit sorting processes.

We aim to increase mangosteen crop yields, improve fruit quality, reduce waste, and support sustainable farming practices across the industry.

Key Features

Disease Detection

Our advanced AI system analyzes images of mangosteen plants and fruits to identify common diseases with high accuracy. Simply upload a photo, and our system will provide a diagnosis within seconds.

Automated Fruit Sorting

Our intelligent sorting system classifies mangosteen fruits based on size, ripeness, and quality. This technology helps producers streamline their operations and ensure consistent product quality.
